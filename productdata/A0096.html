Consultant(s) will develop a CBE Competency Framework for up to three (3) programs based on a common understanding of concepts, vocabulary, methods and tools of CBE curriculum design. This will involve Blackboard working with existing course/learning module material (syllabi and assessments) to reconstruct the courses/learning modules into a CBE framework. Blackboard will facilitate workshops with program instructors, SMEs, and other key stakeholders. (Combination of STS and Educational Services/Course Development)
