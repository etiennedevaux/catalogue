Blackboard has always been a leader in providing communication tools and we are excited to broaden this topic to the Collaborate Ultra experience. This fully asynchronous course is designed for the busy professional who needs a flexible way to quickly get up to speed using Collaborate with the Ultra experience. You’ll also have access to an expert Blackboard consultant who will help guide you through the course and answer questions along the way. Participants will be able to practice suggested tasks in a Collaborate practice room, and you’ll have opportunities for knowledge checks throughout the course to solidify your understanding of the platform and web conferencing.
