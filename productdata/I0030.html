This service provides institutions with tools to integrate and synchronize the Blackboard Learn Calendars with other systems or platforms like: Student Information Systems, Student Timetable Systems, Email or Legacy Systems, providing a unified vision of the Calendar Events within Blackboard Learn. It provides students with tools that allows them to synchronize or subscribe to their Blackboard Personal Calendar with Calendars managed in a different platform. It also provides instructors with tools that allows them to manage the Blackboard Course Calendar and synchronize the Course Calendar with calendars managed in a different platform.
