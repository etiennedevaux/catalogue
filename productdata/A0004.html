Designed for: Organizational leaders, IT managers, financial managers and business analysts

Gain critical insights about your student lifecycle by engaging technical and functional stakeholders throughout the implementation process of Blackboard’s Student Management Solution. Participants will also learn best practices for analyzing existing student data to attract and retain future students.

Our comprehensive approach is designed to help your institution successfully deploy the solution, identify your institution’s unique needs and optimize your strategic analytics investment.

Key stakeholders will include subject matter experts (Admissions, Institutional Research, Enrollment, Accounts Receivable, Registrar, Student Success), business analysts, power users, functional team leads, SIS DBAs and institutional leaders.

Blackboard Product(s): Blackboard IntelligenceTM Student Management Module
