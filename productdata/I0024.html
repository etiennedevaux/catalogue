I0024:Collaborate Adoption Service
Implementing a new technology in an educational setting is always challenging. While many will embrace new ways of doing things, others resist change. Blackboard has found that institutions that regularly assess progress, and reorganise their adoption efforts as needed, gain an advantage in recognising a return on their efforts more rapidly than if they wait for adoption to take its own natural course.

Designed for: System administrators, learning support staff and learning technologists

Optimize your investment in Blackboard Collaborate by assessing the strengths and weaknesses of your institutional implementation of the solution.

Through a series of remote evaluations of your Collaborate environment, Blackboard consultants will assess the adoption rate and effectiveness of the solution at your institution, providing insightful benchmarking against your peers. Assessment is also determined through interviews, an interactive forum and self-evaluation.
