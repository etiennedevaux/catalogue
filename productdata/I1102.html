I1102:Ally and Accessibility Instructor Training
Ally Instructor training provides the knowledge faculty and staff need to unlock the power of Ally. The training offers an in-depth look at accessibility issues and solutions in online learning. Participants will explore crucial barriers to accessibility, and experience first-hand how assistive technologies enable learnes with disabilities to take full advantage of eductaional materials.

The Accessibility Fundamentals for Instructors and Course Designers Training Materials License offers clients the flexibility to install the online course on their own server. Moreover, the course can be branded and customized to meet institutional needs. The course is designed to be non-facilitated meaning the client will not need to train or assign a “teacher.” However it will be interactive with graded activities, practice activities, assessment, and a Certificate of Completion. Clients who choose to deploy the course in a facilitated format will receive guidance on how to do that during the onboarding meetings with a Blackboard Training Consultant.
