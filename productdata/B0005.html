B0005:Learning Essentials Self-Hosted
The 'Learning Essentials' soluiton for Self-Hosted clients provides clients with the full Learn package and Collaborate Web Conferencing. The packages aimed at the North American market include a full range of services in the Year One version, with the 'Continuation' version hvaing some recurring services. The International versions does not have a standard set of services built-in, as these vary from market to market, but recommended services are listed below.
