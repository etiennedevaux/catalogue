This is a combination strategic and functional consulting engagement. Consultant(s) will provide a multi-day, hands-on workshop centered on understanding CBE courses/ learning modules and facilitating learning within CBE programs. Blackboard will work directly with client instructors, SMEs and/or instructional designers to align thinking around a CBE-centered learning, guide participants toward developing and adopting CBE-appropriate practices and instructional design methodologies, and establishing a strategic foundation to sustain and grow CBE efforts over time. (Combination of STS and Educational Services/Course Development)
