The Training Program Development solution, TPD, is a comprehensive professional development package that provides organizations with the resources needed to build a self-sustaining training program at their institution. This allows an organization’s master training team to focus on training and support instead of the development and maintenance of materials. The solution is designed to provide current and accurate training and support material that an organization’s master instructional team can use to train an unlimited number of their learning constituents. TPD materials facilitated from an organization’s Learn and/or Collaborate instance by their master instructional team, allowing an organization the freedom to rebrand and modify the materials to meet their needs.
