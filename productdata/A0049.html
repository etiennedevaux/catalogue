The Teaching and Administration for Collaborate Web Conferencing workshop is designed to help participants build a solid foundation and understanding of the Collaborate product. The workshop uses a modular training format that allows clients to create a targeted experience for participants based on their training and implementation needs. Collaborate Ultra is being developed following a continuous release methodology. This means that new features and functionality are being added to the product. As such, new training modules will be added to this workshop to stay synchronized with the product. Clients will have a total of four workshops – choose one or two administration workshops for a small audience who will manage the administrative side of Collaborate, and two or three end-user workshops for up to 15 participants. A Blackboard Consultant will work with the client to help select the appropriate modules to ensure that the learning objectives fit the client’s needs. This workshop is suitable for clients with up to 2,000 end users.
